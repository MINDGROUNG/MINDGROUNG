Mindful Life Benefits

‘Meditation’ means to being fully present in the “here and now” moment and reaching a state of calmness in your mind. Why do we have to ‘meditate’?

01. Stabilizing our nerve system

Meditation stabilizes and relaxes the sympathetic nervous system that increases brain
fatigue and tension the parasympathetic nervous system that helps the metabolism
smoothly is activated. We will find a peaceful body and mind with stable heartbeat, brain
wave activity, and relaxed muscle through meditation on regular basis.

02. Increase the happy hormone

t stimulates the serotonin hormone that regulates daily vitality, energy, and calmness, and
our cognitive and learning abilities. When we meditate, our body releases ‘dopamine’
which induces enthusiasm and positive perception and ‘oxytocin’ that helps relieve pain
and tension. These hormones are essential and keys to building happy relationships and
productive work life.

03. Activation on our healthy brains

It finally leads to a state of stability, free from the ups and downs of emotions, and
emotional distractions. The gray matter of our brains starts increasing the functions of
information processing, memory, concentration, thinking, language, and consciousness
are strengthened accordingly.
